Submission Policy

To maintain a safe and inclusive environment for the San Francisco community, all submissions must adhere to the following guidelines. Content that violates these rules will not be displayed.

Authenticity & Ownership

  • Photos of artwork not created by the submitter.
  • Stock images or placeholders.
  • Screenshots from digital devices or social media.
  • Stock images of artwork not created by the submitter.

Safety & Sensitive Content

  • Images containing major violence, tragedy, or gore.
  • Explicit sexual themes or imagery.
  • Content depicting self-harm or suicide.
  • Images containing hate symbols, hate speech, or slurs.

Privacy & Legal

  • Images of living people (other than the submitter) where consent is unclear.
  • Photos taken in private spaces that do not belong to the submitter.
  • Political themes intended as propaganda.
  • Criminal themes, including the brandishing of weapons or use of drugs illegal in California.
